Tracker Whacker

Tracker Whacker is a email-forwarding service that removes tracking redirect links from your email.

Users can forward emails to Tracker Whacker and receive a response within two minutes with all of the content of the original email but with all redirect tracking links removed. All link removal is automated, and emails are deleted upon completion. Users are currently limited to 100 forwarded emails per month.

Learn more about email tracking in this guide from the Electronic Frontier Foundation
